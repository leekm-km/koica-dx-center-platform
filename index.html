About the Program

KOICA Digital Transformation Center building ICT capacity in Dumaguete, Philippines

Mission & Vision

The KOICA DX Center is committed to creating sustainable digital transformation in the Philippines by providing comprehensive ICT training to local youth and students.

Our program directly contributes to the UN Sustainable Development Goals:

  • SDG 4 Quality Education - World-class AI and data analytics curriculum
  • SDG 8 Decent Work - Direct employment linkage with BPO companies
